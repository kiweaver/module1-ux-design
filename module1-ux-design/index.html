The Larder

The larder is a place where historically people used to keep food in the coldest room in the house. In New Zealand it was south facing. The building that The Larder cafe is located in, used to be an old home and it happens to face south. It seemed to fit because it's a place where you go and get food. The sister business, Myrtle Bakery, provides delicious bakery goods at The Larder too.

About Us

Local Miramar residents Jacob Brown and Sarah Bullock are the co-owners of the Miramar eatery The Larder. Their vision is to serve the community with fine, honest food in a relaxed neighbourhood surrounding. Their team aim to provide warm and friendly service that is at the same time both professional and knowledgeable. The team at The Larder work passionately to establish and maintain close personal relationships with their growers and suppliers.They endeavour to source food locally and directly from the farmers. Jacob believes that as well as supporting small local business, it also results in produce that has wonderful flavour and integrity. The menu therefore is produce-driven and directly reflective of the seasons. Jacob and Sarah believe strongly in the philosophy of 'nose to tail eating' where all the parts of the animal are utilised and honoured.
